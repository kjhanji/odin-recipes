Egg Wrap

Description

Quick and easy snack great for protein and people in a hurry.

Ingredients

  • tortilla wrap
  • eggs
  • butter
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Warm tortilla in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Melt butter in a pan.Scramble eggs until almost set, about 2 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle cheese all over the eggs. Once melted transfer eggs into the tortilla.
  4. Enjoy!
